Radisson Hotel, Varanasi


Location : The Mall Rd, Varanasi cantonment, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh 221002

Check-in time : 14:00 hrs

Check-out time : 12:00 hrs

A 7-minute walk from a bus stop, this upscale hotel is also 5 km from Kashi Vishwanath Temple and 9 km from Banaras Hindu University.

Offering free Wi-Fi, the modern rooms have flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add sitting areas and access to a lounge offering free beverages, while suites have living rooms with extra TVs. Room service is available.

Complimentary amenities include a breakfast buffet and parking. There's a casual restaurant and a cafe, plus a coffee shop and a cosy bar. Additional perks include an outdoor pool, a hot tub and a gym, as well as a spa with massages (fee).

Alka Hotel, Varanasi


Location : D3/23 Meer Ghat, near Golden Temple, 221001

Check-in time : 12:30 hrs

Check-out time : 11:00 hrs

076190 09995

Overlooking the banks of the Ganges river, this modest hotel is 5 minutes' walk from the 18th-century Kashi Vishwanath Temple and 7 minutes on foot from the Gyanvapi Mosque.

Basic rooms feature TVs and shared bathrooms. Upgrades add private bathrooms and air-conditioning; some have balconies with river views.

A vegetarian restaurant offers open-air dining on a terrace. There's also a garden and an Ayurvedic spa area.

Palace on Steps, Varanasi


Location : Rana Mahal Ghat, D21 / 11, Bangali Tola Rd, near Dashaswamedh Ghat, Bangali Tola, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh 221001

Check-in time : 13:00 hrs

Check-out time : 11:00 hrs

0542 245 0970

This straightforward budget hotel on the banks of the Ganges River lies 5 minutes' walk from the Dashashwamedh Ghat staircase, a 9-minute walk from the famous Shri Kashi Vishwanth Temple and 5 km from Banaras Hindu University.

Featuring balconies with river views, the scaled-back rooms provide free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and fans. Upgrades add refined touches such as decorative moulding, circular beds and/or tufted headboards. Room service is available 24/7.

A breakfast buffet is complimentary. There's an airy, casual restaurant offering international cuisine. A rooftop lounge overlooks the river. A doctor is on call.
