Top 5 hotels in Andaman and Nicobar Islands

1.

Barefoot at Havelock

One of the best hotels in Andaman’s is the Barefoot at Havelock where you can stay at luxurious cottage and villas in the midst of nature with a view of the sea.
What sets this place apart from the other hotels in Havelock Island is the unique Ayurvedic treatment at the Barefoot Ayurvedic Wellness Centre.


Check out the hotel

2.

Munjoh Ocean Resort

Imagine waking up in the morning to sound of the waves and the first sight of swaying coconut trees.
Isn’t it amazing! This is exactly what Munjoh Ocean resort offers with its luxurious villas with exclusive patios and the best part, it is close to the best tourist spots.

Check out the hotel

3.

Fortune Resort Bay Island

Very few places in Andaman’s can give you the luxury of a beach and a forest, together.
Fortune Resort Bay, located on the edge of virgin rainforests combines the best of both worlds and creates a fun and exciting experience for you.

Check out the hotel

4.

Aastha Eco Resort

Located in Little Andaman, this resort gives you a taste of the local life with its bamboo and brick wall hut accommodation.
A boom in Andaman tourism has been thanks to the fact that it caters to everyone, and if you are travelling on a budget, check out the accommodations at this amazing Eco resort, which is one of the best hotels in Andaman.

Check out the hotel

5.

The Wild Orchid Resort

If you are in for luxurious stay surrounded by simplicity in the lap of nature, then this is one of the best hotels in Andaman to choose from.
The spacious cottages are built in traditional Andaman style with thatched roofs, bamboo canes to make sure they co-exist with the eco system.

Check out the hotel
